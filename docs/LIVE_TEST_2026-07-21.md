## Product observations

The live result was evidence-bound, concrete, and reversible. It recommended a
10% stage with a named rollback owner, an Android crash-free stop threshold, a
staffed observation window, and use of the campaign waitlist if evidence remained
poor. Seven of ten adjudicated claims survived and three were unsupported.

One weakness surfaced: the arbiter preserved an unresolved tension but classified
no atomic claim as disputed. This is not a schema failure, but it is an evaluation
target for prompt calibration; future tests should measure whether important
unresolved tensions reliably appear in both the narrative and claim statuses.
